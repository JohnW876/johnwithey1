ARTIST'S BIO

Graduating from Leeds Beckett University in 2009 following a successful diploma at Leeds Arts University, John went to work in a photography studio and gradually built up the skills to become a professional photographer. At the same time he never lost his love of painting, printmaking and illustration and continued to produce work based around themes of abstraction, fragmentation and energy.

SERVICES

John has been a professional photographer for over a decade and has created illustrations and artwork for numerous projects including album covers, posters, websites and magazines.

PHOTOGRAPHY

In the studio John has specialised in product photography developing a style with bold colours and impactful images that tell a story. His street photography has featured in a number of publications and an upcoming exhibition of his work will be announced shortly. If you have a project in mind and would like to discuss your requirements from pack shots to full ad campaigns, you can reach us via our contact page.

ARTWORK

John specialises in abstract designs which are available to license for any project. Please get in touch to discuss via our contact page.
